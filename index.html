Tomohiro's Memorial Mountains

My hobby is hiking mountains. I have hiked a lot of mountains. I introduce my memorial 3 mountains.

1. Mt. Tateyama

Mt. Tateyama is one of the most famous mountains in Japan. It is 3,015 meters high. It is located in the northern part of Hida mountains. I stayed in a tent to see the stars at night. The view of the stars was very beautiful. I could see the Milky Way for the first time.

2. Mt. Hakuba

Mt. Hakuba is famous for snowy valley in Japan. We can see the snow and walk on the snow valley in Summer. I wanted to hike on the snow valley. The view of the snow valley was very beautiful. The experience was excited and valuable.

3. Karasawa Cirque

Mt. Karasawa Cirque has very beautiful autumn leaves view. It was my dream to see the view. I hiked the mountain at September late and stayed in a tent. It was very cold at night, but the view was very beautiful.
